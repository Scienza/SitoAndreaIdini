I accept master and bachelor thesis projects, spanning my research interests.
The available projects concern:

  • modelling and computation of the properties of the atomic nucleus with Green functions and DFT,
  • optimization and statistical algorithms (including machine learning) for nuclear physics and computer vision,
  • quantum computing of many-body systems,
  • application of chaos theory to geography of urbanization and econophysics (suggested for bachelor).
Also, Have a look at my and publications and see if you find something appealing. Feel free and encouraged to propose your own project.
During the course of the thesis I put emphasis in developing physical intuition but also developer skills, in accordance with student predisposition. You are encouraged and openly communicate your aims when starting the thesis project.
Have also a look at the matfys guidelines for thesis .
