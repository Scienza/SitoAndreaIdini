About me

I am Andrea Idini, an Associate Senior Lecturer at Lund University, in the division of mathematical physics. My research interests mainly concern nuclear structure and reactions, with a particular emphasis on the limits of many-body approaches.
However, I have the tendency of biting more than I can chew, as you can see from the summary and list of projects. Among these, I collaborate with Scape technologies working on computer vision and 3D reconstruction.
To know more about my past, have a look at my papers , or at my cv.
